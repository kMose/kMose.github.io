To get to valorant

CS:GO sensitivity divided by 3.3735
Overwatch sensitivity divided by 10.6
Apex Legends sensitivity divided by 3.18181818
Rainbow Six sensitivity multiplied by 1.2



CSGO CM/360 CALC

(360 / (y * d * w * s)) * 2.54

y = m_yaw (0.022 default)

d = DPI

w = windows sensitivity multiplier (1 by default)

s = in-game sensitivity
